The Agents

Three agents. Infinite possibilities.

Each agent is named, branded, and configured entirely around your business. Here is what they are built to do.

01
Agent 01
Lead Qualification

Named and branded for your business

Agent 01 is your first point of contact with every prospect who reaches out to your business. The moment an inquiry arrives, through your website, your social media, your ads, or any connected channel, Agent 01 engages immediately. It asks the questions your business needs answered before investing time in a prospect. It filters out the noise, identifies genuine intent, and delivers only the leads that meet your criteria directly to your team. It runs without breaks, without moods, and without missing a single inquiry regardless of the time or volume.

Instant response to every inbound inquiry regardless of time or volume, so no prospect ever waits.

Custom qualification criteria built entirely around your business requirements, not generic templates.

Multi-channel deployment across your website, social platforms, and ad campaigns from a single configuration.

Intelligent lead scoring based on your defined parameters, so your team focuses only on the highest value prospects.

Seamless handoff to your team or directly to Agent 02 the moment a lead meets your qualification threshold.

Full conversation logging for every interaction, giving your team complete context before the first human touch.

In Practice

A home services company running ads across three platforms receives 40 inquiries over a weekend. Agent 01 engages every single one within seconds, asks about service type, location, timeline, and budget, and by Monday morning the owner has a shortlist of 12 qualified prospects ready for a callback. The other 28 were filtered out automatically.

02
Agent 02
Operations & Coordination

Named and branded for your business

Agent 02 is your operations layer. Once a lead is qualified, Agent 02 takes ownership of the relationship and moves it through your pipeline without requiring your team to track, chase, or remember anything. It manages milestones, sends reminders, triggers follow ups at the right moments, and delivers scheduled briefings so your team always knows exactly where every active prospect stands. Nothing falls through. Nothing gets forgotten. Your pipeline runs itself.

Automated milestone creation and tracking for every active prospect, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Proactive alerts when deadlines or follow up windows approach, keeping your team ahead of every deal.

Scheduled daily or weekly briefings delivered directly to your team with a complete picture of your pipeline.

Fully configurable pipeline stages that match your exact business process, not a one size fits all template.

Automatic follow up sequences triggered by prospect behavior, ensuring timely contact without manual intervention.

Complete audit trail of every action taken on every prospect, giving your team full visibility and accountability.

In Practice

A financial advisory firm with 30 active prospects in various stages of onboarding deploys Agent 02 to manage the pipeline. Every morning the lead advisor receives a briefing listing which clients need contact that day, which documents are outstanding, and which deadlines are approaching. No spreadsheet. No assistant. No dropped balls.

03
Agent 03
Market Intelligence

Named and branded for your business

Agent 03 is your presence engine. While your team focuses on closing, Agent 03 ensures your brand never goes quiet. It generates intelligent content and market reports relevant to your industry and distributes them to your sphere of influence on a schedule you define. It keeps your name in front of the people who matter, maintains relationships between transactions, and ensures that when someone in your network is ready to buy, your name is the first one they think of.

Automated generation of market and industry intelligence relevant to your business, keeping your content always current.

Scheduled distribution to your full sphere of influence on a cadence you define, without any manual effort.

Custom content tone and focus configured to your brand voice, so every communication feels like it came from you.

Segmented outreach based on contact type or relationship stage, ensuring the right message reaches the right person.

Performance tracking on open rates and engagement, so you can see exactly how your sphere is responding.

Fully hands off operation after initial configuration, requiring no ongoing maintenance from your team.

In Practice

A boutique insurance agency configures Agent 03 to send a monthly industry update to their full client database of 800 contacts. The report covers relevant regulatory changes, market trends, and renewal reminders. Open rates average 34 percent. Three clients respond each month with new policy inquiries they had been meaning to bring up. The agency principal spends zero time on it.
